Pull-Up-Bars

A pull-up is an upper-body strength exercise. The pull-up is a closed-chain movement where the body is suspended by the hands and pulls up. As this happens, the elbows flex and the shoulders adduct and extend to bring the elbows to the torso. Pull-ups are a common way to measure the upper body strength of the "pulling muscles".

Specifications

  • 2.03 solid stainless steel bar (34-35mm)
  • 2 x 3.7M (100mm x 100mm x 4mm) Galvanised posts
  • 2 Galvanised Post caps
  • 0.6m3 concrete mix (25MPA X 14mm Stones)

Hole Dimensions

  • 500mm x 500mm x 1.2m deep
  • Distance between posts (inside) 1.7m
  • Distance from ground to bar is 2.5m (Plenty of room for legs to hang even if >6" tall)

Variations

  • Standard
  • Weighted
  • Behind-the-neck pull-up
  • One arm
  • Extended-leg pull-up
  • Sternum chins

Advantages

  • Strengthen the back muscles
  • Strengthen the arm and shoulder muscles
  • Improve grip strength
  • Improve overall body strength and fitness level
  • Improve physical and mental health
